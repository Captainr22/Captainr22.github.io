Research

I am fascinated by natural language processing🤖💭 and curious about the capabilities of large language models (LLMs) and underlying mechanisms 🔍. Currently, I focus on reasoning of LLMs (eg., reasoning ability evolution, reinforcement alignment and efficient reasoning). Concurrently, I am researching the application of LLMs in vertical domains, with an emphasis on Agents. Representative papers are as follows.
